Logging Support Issues
If you have a support issue that requires assistance, please send an email to support@seamcor.com to log the issue. The appropriate team at Seamcor will receive notification about your support request and the ticket will be logged in our internal support system. Your support request will be dealt with in line with your support agreement.

Hours of Operation
Standard Support: Monday-Friday, 08:30-17:00 (excl. UK bank holidays)
Premium Support: 7 days a week, 08:00-20:00 (excl. Christmas Day)
Fully Managed: Monday-Friday, 08:30-17:00 (excl. UK bank holidays)
SOTI: Same as support package
