# Data Processing Agreement — Seamcor Platform

**Between:** Customer and Seamvex Data Systems Ltd, trading as Seamcor

**Template version:** June 2026  
**Use with:** Customer software agreement. Leave customer details blank until signing.

---

## When to use this agreement

Seamvex **does not host** customer platform data. Customers run Seamcor **on their own servers** (on-premises or private cloud).

| Situation | Attach this agreement? |
|-----------|------------------------|
| Customer hosts the platform; Seamvex has **no access** to live production data | **No** |
| **Fully Managed** — Seamvex installs, configures, or supports the platform with **access to production data** | **Yes** |

The customer software agreement already includes processor terms where Seamvex handles personal data on the customer’s behalf. Use this standalone agreement when **Fully Managed** services apply — including a **site licence including Fully Managed** order.

## 1. Background

1.1 The Controller uses the Seamcor software platform (the “**Platform**”) under a software agreement with the Processor.

1.2 Where the Processor handles personal data on the Controller’s behalf during **Fully Managed** installation, configuration, or support with access to production systems, this Data Processing Agreement (“**Agreement**”) sets out how each party will meet UK data protection law.

1.3 This Agreement supplements the software agreement. If there is a conflict about data protection, this Agreement takes precedence over the software agreement.

---

## 2. Definitions

**“Data Protection Legislation”** means the UK GDPR, the Data Protection Act 2018, and related legislation as amended.

**“Personal Data”**, **“Processing”**, **“Controller”**, **“Processor”**, **“Data Subject”**, and **“Personal Data Breach”** have the meanings given in the Data Protection Legislation.

**“Platform Data”** means Personal Data that the Processor handles on behalf of the Controller through or in connection with the Platform during Fully Managed services.

---

## 3. Roles

3.1 The Controller decides why and how Platform Data is used and is the **Controller** of Platform Data.

3.2 The Processor handles Platform Data only on documented instructions from the Controller, as set out in this Agreement and the software agreement.

---

## 4. Details of processing

| Item | Detail |
|------|--------|
| Subject matter | Installation, configuration, support, and maintenance of the Platform |
| Duration | Term of the software agreement and any support period |
| Nature and purpose | Fully Managed services where Processor staff may access Platform Data in production systems |
| Types of personal data | As uploaded by the Controller — typically names, roles, and audit or compliance records visible during support |
| Categories of data subjects | Controller’s employees, contractors, and authorised users |
| Special category data | Only if the Controller uploads it |

---

## 5. What Seamvex will do (Processor obligations)

The Processor shall:

5.1 handle Platform Data only on documented instructions from the Controller, unless UK law requires otherwise;

5.2 ensure people authorised to handle Platform Data are bound by confidentiality;

5.3 put in place appropriate technical and organisational security measures (Article 32 UK GDPR);

5.4 not use another processor (sub-processor) without the Controller’s general written authorisation (see Schedule 1);

5.5 help the Controller respond to data subject requests and impact assessments where reasonably possible;

5.6 tell the Controller without undue delay after becoming aware of a Personal Data Breach;

5.7 delete or return Platform Data when services end, unless law requires retention;

5.8 provide information needed to show compliance and allow audits on reasonable notice (no more than once per year unless a regulator requires more).

---

## 6. What the customer will do (Controller obligations)

The Controller shall:

6.1 ensure it has a lawful basis to upload Platform Data and to instruct the Processor;

6.2 ensure its privacy notices cover use of the Platform;

6.3 not upload unnecessary special category data without appropriate safeguards;

6.4 comply with Data Protection Legislation in its use of the Platform.

---

## 7. International transfers

7.1 The Processor shall not transfer Platform Data outside the UK or EEA without appropriate safeguards, unless an adequacy decision applies.

7.2 Current sub-processors are listed in Schedule 1. The Processor shall tell the Controller about intended changes with reasonable notice.

---

## 8. Liability

8.1 Liability under this Agreement is subject to the limitation of liability in the software agreement, except where liability cannot be limited by law.

---

## 9. Term

9.1 This Agreement applies for the term of the software agreement and until Platform Data is deleted or returned.

---

## 10. Law

10.1 This Agreement is governed by the laws of England and Wales.
