## Privacy Statement

### Who we are

**Seamvex Data Systems Ltd** (company number 17188046), trading as **Seamcor**, provides the Seamcor software platform. When you use Seamcor at the request of your employer or organisation, that organisation is the **data controller** and Seamvex is a **data processor** (for Fully Managed deployments only).

Contact: accounts@seamcor.com · support@seamcor.com

### What data we process

Depending on how your organisation uses Seamcor and whether Fully Managed support applies, we may process:

- Names, email addresses, and job roles  
- Login and audit trail data visible during support  
- Compliance, quality, and operational records your organisation uploads  
- Technical logs needed to operate and secure the platform during support  

We do not sell personal data.

### Why we process it

We process data to provide, support, and secure the Seamcor platform under our contract with your organisation — during Fully Managed installation, configuration, and support.

### How long we keep it

For the duration of your organisation’s agreement and as instructed by your organisation, subject to backup and legal retention requirements.

### Your rights

Contact **your organisation** (the controller) first to exercise access, correction, erasure, or other rights. We will assist your organisation as required by our DPA.

You may complain to the ICO: https://ico.org.uk/make-a-complaint/

### Security

We use appropriate technical and organisational measures to protect personal data. No system is completely secure; your organisation controls what data is uploaded and where it is hosted.

### International transfers

If data is processed outside the UK/EEA, we use appropriate safeguards as described in our DPA with your organisation.

### Changes

We may update this statement. Material changes will be communicated via your organisation or through the platform where appropriate.
